Meet The Team

Sofia Patterson

Teacher

Sofia joined a ceramics class on a whim 4 years ago and hasn't looked back! After attending a few workshops, she decided to study for a Ceramics MA at Staffordshire university and is now a full time potter. Mugs and plant pots are her favourite things to make, and she loves experimenting with texture in her designs.

Michael Haas

Teacher & Studio Technician

Michael discovered his love for pottery here at Social Ceramics after attending classes as a way to get in touch with his creativity. Coming from a background in hospitality, Michael now combines all his creative and technical skills with his amazing personality and keeps us up to date on all things technical about ceramics and the studio!

Stella Figueroa

Teacher

Stella was introduced to ceramics on a team-building event at work and decided to keep up the practice after reaping the therapeutic benefits of clay! She produces work at the studio part-time and wants to pass her passion onto others at Social Ceramics! Experimenting with shape and movement, Stella loves creating decorative, abstract pieces.

Nora Ahmed

Teacher

Nora discovered her love of ceramics in college where she studied Art & Design. Finding the ceramics module to be too short, she pursued it as a hobby by taking classes with a friend in her spare time. Nora has explored the world, finding inspiration and collecting ceramics from her travels. Nora creates amazing, intricate pieces, and you'll find her experimenting with glazes and slip designs.
